About Us

White Glove moving Solution started with one guy and one truck but it developed into a wide range of services moving company based in Los Angeles California and delivering to all the lower 48 states. Our workers have more than 10 years of moving experience and are professionally trained to pack and deliver any items no matter the size or fragility. We handle situations others can’t, because we are experience, flexible and creative. Either it’s a vintage piano or a modern piece of art, a grandmother’s vase or a whole office – we know how to take care of your very important things. If you are about to relocate just a few items or you need to move from a huge three-storey mansion to even a bigger one – we’ve got you covered.
